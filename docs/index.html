About Team Trinity

Joseph and Grace Seko formed Team Trinity under the mentorship of 300 Leadership Development to spread financial knowledge to individuals and families in order to help them protect their futures and prepare for financial freedom. They live in the San Francisco East Bay Area, where they raise their 7 kids, run a successful rental business, while leading a growing team of like-minded financial professionals (Team TRINITY) on the same mission to not leave any family behind.

Team TRINITY is committed to helping as many families as we can with life insurance, retirement, financial strategies, and free educational events.

Looking forward to the future and working with you and anyone who would benefit from this knowledge!
